Roots That Make Us Different

We trigger critical thinking skills in our coders.

The fundamentals instilled by Fantasy Coders Academy over time leads to accelerated learning throughout the lives of our coders. While you pick up the fundamentals, you will also be triggered to think critcally in solving problems that you may encounter while learning how to code. With critical thinking ingrained in you, you can expect to gain opportunities in developing applications for the real world.

We hone your knowledge at your own pace.

We recognize that each individual progresses at different pace and some may need more time to process and reflect at each stage of learning. At Fantasy Coders Academy, we accomodate to your learning pace by constantly asking you questions to trigger your personal reflection, as well as to enable you to share with us where are some of your weaknesses in which we can focus more on. By building the rapport between you and us, we can make your learning more enjoyable.

We train your self confidence in solving challenges.

Confidence is key in order to make better applications for the real world. Without confidence, one will not have the motivation nor trust in oneself that he/she has the ability to code an impressive and unique application, At Fantasy Coders Academy, we ensure that you will grow to be independent and confident coders by ensuring that you go through the various thinking processes before relying on us for help and guidance. Along the way, we will also reward you if you can solve challenges on your own.
